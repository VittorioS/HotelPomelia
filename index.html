A Family Commitment to a Greener Future

Welcome to Hotel Pomelia, where comfort meets sustainability.
As a family-run hotel, we take pride in managing our establishment with love and care;
creating a warm and welcoming atmosphere for every guest. For years, we have dedicated ourselves to not only providing exceptional service but also to promoting eco-friendly practices. Our commitment to a green future drives every decision we make—from sourcing local, organic ingredients for our kitchen to implementing energy-efficient systems throughout our hotel.

Farm-to-Table Dining

At Hotel Pomelia, we believe that the essence of exceptional dining lies in the quality of ingredients. That’s why we are proud to serve authentic Italian cuisine made from organic ingredients sourced directly from our own garden. Our culinary team is passionate about creating dishes that not only taste delightful but also reflect our commitment to sustainability and health.

Beach Activities and Exclusive Tours at Hotel Pomelia

At Hotel Pomelia, we pride ourselves on offering an unforgettable beach experience just steps away from your room. Our serene private beach is the perfect getaway for relaxation and adventure, with soft white sand and crystal-clear waters. Whether you're looking to unwind or seek some excitement, we have activities and tours tailored just for you.
